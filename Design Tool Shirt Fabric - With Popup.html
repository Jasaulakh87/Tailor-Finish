Rivington Grey Micro Check
$125

Woven and indigo dyed in Japan, this chambray fabric is the real deal. It has a subtle printed folk stripe that really ups the ante on a timeless casual design. Made in small batches on shuttle looms, a technique pioneered in America but perfected by Japanese mills, the slightly uneven weave gives this light indigo chambray incredible visual texture. The indigo dye will also fade out over time, meaning this shirt gets better the more you wear it.

  • Weave: Denim/Chambray
  • Thread Count: 40s
  • Thickness: Lightweight
  • Opacity Rating: Very Opaque
  • Fabric ID: PCF1826
