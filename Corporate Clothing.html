Corporate clothing

Three simple steps to corporate clothing that actually fits your staff.

  • Quote & design proposal within 24 hrs

    Contact us today and get a unique design proposal with a quote within 24 hours.

  • Measuring and orders with no minimum quantity

    Yes, you read that right! No minimum quantity for all orders. Get your unique made-to-measure, or adapted from custom sizes orders today.

  • Fast delivery in 10-20 days

    After ordering, our tailors need some time for cutting, sewing and embroidering your company’s unique design.

Four good reasons to choose made-to-measure corporate clothing:

  • Our production line is Co2 neutral. Good for nature and an added value to your company.
  • We guarantee a perfect fit and individual alterations according to your needs.
  • You choose the fabric, collar, buttons, embroidery and other detailing - with personal B2B-assistance, of course.
  • We own the tailor shop and deliver individual garments of full collections within 10-20 days.

Contact us, and let us tell you how we can design and tailor your company’s next collection of corporate clothing.
